Kayley Clark (vertical)

The "vh" changes the viewport to be relative to its height. The 200% in the h1 is then relative to its parent (vh in div).

Kayley Clark (horizontal)

The "vw" changes the viewport to be relative to its width. 200% in the h1 is then connected to it's parent as well.
